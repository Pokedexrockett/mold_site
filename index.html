Well, that is what I hope to change here. I'm going to present you with the facts of why/how mold spreads on cannabis, how to spot it, and ways to prevent it from happening in the first place.

How do you spot mold on cannabis?

Spotting mold on cannabis can be tricky if it's in the early stages, and is very noticible during the Xenomorph hatching, as I like to put it. Mold on cannabis will often be white in color and look similar to spiders silk. Don't be fooled. It is not extra kiefy and won't get you more high. It will actually make you wuite sick. Colas, or the very top buds are the most succeptable to mold being they are the largest buds on the plant. And with them being the biggest, they hold the most amount of water. When searching for mold in any bud it is important to gently open the bud up so as to spread as little to no mold spores. They can and will infect your other plants.
