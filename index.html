Cannabis and Mold

Did you know that cannabis can grow mold?
Unfortunately, a lot of people don't and are therefore unknowingly consuming contaminated bud. A lot of people haven't even seen what moldy weed looks like.
Well, that is what I hope to change here. I'm going to present you with the facts of why/how mold spreads on cannabis, how to spot it, and ways to prevent it from happening in the first place.

Why/how does cannabis grow mold?

Cannabis can mold for a variety of reasons including
*Humidity
*Heat
*Lack of air flow
*Impropper storage
*Outside contaminents

How do you spot mold on cannabis?

Spotting mold can be tricky, but there are signs to look out for
*White, cottony, silky like supstance like spiders web can be seen
*It is often found close to the stem
*Look in larger buds such as colas
*Mold can turn black if conditions are really bad

How do you prevent mold on cannabis?

Preventing mold can be very simple if the propper steps are followed
*Always check to make sure humidity levels are accurate
*Ensure plants have enough room apart from one another to dry properly
*Burp containers storing dried buds regularly
